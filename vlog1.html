travel information

Mysore is a city in Karnataka, India. Mysore is 140 kilometres (87 miles) south-west of Bangalore. Travel time from kerala to Mysore is 8-10 hours by road or rail when it by air it would take only less than 1 and half hours. Mysore is the second largest city in Karnataka. Mysore Palace, Jaganmohan Palace, Chamundi Hills, Brindavan Gardens, and Mysore Zoo are the places to see in Mysore.
